---
type: Theorem
id: thm-main
name: "Main Result"
---

For every instance of the asymmetric traveling salesman problem,
there is a polynomial-time randomised algorithm that, with high
probability, returns a tour of cost at most $\\alpha \\cdot
\\text{OPT}$ where $\\alpha$ is a constant strictly less than the
constant established in [@svensson-tarnawski-vegh2018].
