This chapter states the main theorem and gives its proof.

The proof proceeds by analysing a randomised rounding of the
Held–Karp linear programming relaxation [@held-karp1970], using ideas
from the cycle-cover decomposition that has been the workhorse of
ATSP analyses since [@svensson-tarnawski-vegh2018].
