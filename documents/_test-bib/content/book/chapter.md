The first author to argue for transmutation of species, building on the
geological work of his predecessors [@lyell1830], proposed natural
selection as the mechanism [@darwin1859]{page=42}. Independent contemporary
work [@wallace1858; @darwin1859] reached compatible conclusions; Mendel
showed the discrete inheritance these arguments demanded
[@mendel1866]{suppress-author}.
